= 12. Glossary

Status: confirmed/planned terms from Chapter 1, repository policy, C4 model, and KMS feature files.

[cols="1,1,3,2",options="header"]
|===
| Term | Status | Definition | Source / Scope

| Magrathea PKI
| confirmed
| Product for building and operating PKI and trust-management capabilities.
| Chapter 1

| X.509 CA
| confirmed/planned
| Bounded context for X.509 certificate authority capabilities, including issuance, hierarchy, revocation, and lifecycle operations.
| Chapter 1, C4

| CVC CA
| confirmed/planned
| Bounded context for Card Verifiable Certificate capabilities.
| Chapter 1, C4

| SSH CA
| confirmed/planned
| Bounded context for SSH certificate capabilities.
| Chapter 1, C4

| KMS
| planned / feature-informed
| Key Management Service bounded context for key lifecycle, signing, encryption, policy enforcement, audit, and custody backend selection.
| C4, `features/kms`

| HSM Key Custody
| confirmed/planned
| Product-owned or product-managed HSM-backed custody and cryptographic operation bounded context.
| Chapter 1, C4

| TPM Attestation
| confirmed/planned
| Product-owned or product-managed TPM-backed trust, attestation, and key sealing bounded context.
| Chapter 1, C4

| Shared PKI Kernel
| confirmed/planned
| Shared language and behavior for common PKI concerns that do not erase protocol-specific semantics.
| Chapter 1

| Crypto/Key Custody Kernel
| confirmed/planned
| Shared language and behavior for cryptographic keys, key handles, custody references, and backend-neutral custody concepts.
| Chapter 1

| Policy Kernel
| confirmed/planned
| Shared language and behavior for policy rules, allowed usage, approvals, backend requirements, and denial reasons.
| Chapter 1, KMS features

| Audit Kernel
| confirmed/planned
| Shared language and behavior for audit events and compliance evidence.
| Chapter 1, KMS features

| Algorithm taxonomy
| confirmed design
| A closed set of stable intrinsic identities for one protocol role. X.509 subject SPKI profiles, issuer signature suites, provider mechanisms, CVC, SSH, and content hashes are separate taxonomies.
| ADR 0003, `subject-public-key-profile-taxonomy.feature`

| Subject public-key profile
| confirmed design / partially implementation-informed
| Intrinsic X.509 SubjectPublicKeyInfo role identity. The planned Java type name is `SubjectPublicKeyProfile`; current code still uses the bare `PublicKeyAlgorithm` enum with exactly `EC_P256` and `RSA_3072`.
| ADR 0003, `REQ-X509-SUBJECT-PUBLIC-KEY-PROFILE-001`–`005`, episode 0004

| Wire token
| confirmed design
| Explicit immutable case-sensitive protocol/storage identity of an enriched-enum value, independent of Java enum name and ordinal. Current tokens alone do not prove enrichment or deployment eligibility.
| ADR 0003

| Intrinsic algorithm fact
| confirmed design
| Stable fact inherent to a protocol identity, such as a fixed wire token or normative OID. Deployment permission, dates, standards editions, and certification evidence are not intrinsic facts.
| ADR 0003

| Deployment-policy snapshot
| confirmed design / not implemented
| Strictly admitted immutable content-addressed declarative YAML selecting known taxonomy tokens and compatible compiled validators for a profile-, scope-, edition-, and time-specific deployment decision. It remains outside CertificateProfile v1 identity.
| ADR 0004, `deployment-policy-snapshots.feature`

| Validator catalog
| confirmed design / not implemented
| Closed versioned collection of compiled Java validators, each with a stable rule ID, deterministic semantics, typed bounded parameters, and explicit policy-schema compatibility.
| ADR 0004

| Evidence snapshot
| confirmed design / not implemented
| Immutable identified evidence about a concrete provider, module, configuration, standards edition, or deployment state, evaluated for applicability and freshness at an explicit instant.
| ADR 0004

| Evaluation instant
| confirmed design / not implemented
| Caller-supplied time used deterministically for all effective intervals, transition horizons, and evidence-freshness checks; evaluators must not read an implicit wall clock.
| ADR 0004, `REQ-X509-DEPLOYMENT-POLICY-005`

| Governed publication state
| confirmed design / not implemented
| Signed manifest and monotonic namespace state used to authorize activation and reject stale, revoked, expired, or conflicting policy publication; a content hash or Git history alone is not freshness proof.
| ADR 0004, `REQ-X509-DEPLOYMENT-POLICY-009`–`011`

| KeyHandle
| feature-informed
| Identifier returned by KMS to reference a key and retrieve metadata or perform permitted operations.
| `key-lifecycle.feature`

| Key metadata
| feature-informed
| Algorithm, usage, label, state, and related attributes associated with a key.
| `key-lifecycle.feature`

| Key policy
| feature-informed
| Rules that constrain allowed key usage, required backend type, and approvals before use.
| `key-policy.feature`

| Backend type
| feature-informed
| Required or available custody/crypto backend, currently described in requirements as `HSM` or `SOFTWARE`.
| `key-policy.feature`

| Audit event
| feature-informed
| Append-only record of a key operation, policy denial, or audit-related failure.
| `key-audit.feature`

| RA Operator
| confirmed
| Registration Authority operator who validates registration evidence and certificate requests.
| Chapter 1

| CA Operator
| confirmed
| Certificate Authority operator who operates CA lifecycle, issuance policy, revocation, and CA administration.
| Chapter 1

| Compliance Officer
| confirmed
| Stakeholder who reviews evidence for compliance targets and internal controls.
| Chapter 1
|===

== 12.1 Terms Needing Clarification

[cols="1,3",options="header"]
|===
| Term | Clarification Needed

| External customer
| Which product personas and integration modes are required?

| Software key custody
| Which cryptographic provider, storage, wrapping, and rotation rules apply?

| Approval
| Which actor grants approval, how is it authenticated, and how long is it valid?

| Identity
| KMS audit requirements mention identity, but identity source and subject model are not specified.

| Policy snapshot identity
| Canonicalization, digest namespace, identifier grammar, and golden fixtures remain unresolved under `REQ-X509-DEPLOYMENT-POLICY-013`.

| Break-glass rollback
| Authorization, authentication, monotonic-state recovery, and audit evidence remain unresolved under `REQ-X509-DEPLOYMENT-POLICY-015`.
|===
